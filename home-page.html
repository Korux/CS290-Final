Oregon State University is located in the beautiful city of Corvallis. Located in mid-western Oregon and just south of the State captial, OSU is no more than 2 hours away from mountains, the beach, or the city of Portland. Oregon State University is 1 of 2 universities in the United States that has land, sea, space, and sun grants. It was founded in 1868 making this year, the 150 year annivesary of this prestigious university.
